What is nutricalc?

Created in 2019 by Jerry Xia, nutricalc calculates recommended daily values based on age, gender, and daily activity. These values are suggestions and do not take into account all factors, use with discretion. For more information and statistics visit https://health.gov/dietaryguidelines/.
